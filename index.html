Our Mission

We specialize in providing trauma-informed, mentor-focused, and family-like group homes for two distinct groups. Our level 4 foster care homes cater to children with high trauma and additional physical care needs, while our special needs group homes offer support to those with severe behaviors striving to achieve their life goals. With faith as our foundation, we aim to bring honor and glory to God through our work.

Partnership Opportunities: Non-Profits, Vendors, Corporations

We're always looking for partnership opportunities with non-profits, vendors, and corporations who share our commitment to helping children in need.
